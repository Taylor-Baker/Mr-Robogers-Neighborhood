A safe place for robots and counting.

Welcome

Hello, and welcome to Mr. Roboger's neighborhood. There are only three rules in this neighborhood, set down by our neighborhood watch:

  1. All 1s shall be translated into "beep"
  2. All 2s shall be translated into "boop"
  3. All 3s shall be translated into "won't you be my neighbor?"

While it may be cool for the young robot crowd to use such fowl language, here in Mr. Roboger's neighborhood, we won't tolerate such vulger phrasing.
